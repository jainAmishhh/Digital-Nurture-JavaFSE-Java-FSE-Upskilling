Community Bulletin

The Local Community Event Portal continues to bring residents together through various educational, cultural, and social initiatives. This month, volunteers organized a community clean-up drive that attracted participants from different age groups and backgrounds.

In addition to environmental activities, several technology workshops were conducted to help students and professionals improve their digital skills. Experts shared practical knowledge about web development, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

The city council also announced upcoming health awareness campaigns and sports activities aimed at encouraging a healthy lifestyle among citizens. Registration for these events is available through the portal.

Residents are encouraged to participate actively and contribute ideas for future community programs. The portal serves as a platform for collaboration, learning, and social engagement.
